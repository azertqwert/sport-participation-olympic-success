Sport Participation and Olympic Success

Exploring the relationship between access to sport and Olympic performance

Introduction

Sport plays an important social role by contributing to public health, social inclusion, and individual well-being. However, access to sport and opportunities to reach elite performance are not evenly distributed across countries.

This project explores whether higher sport participation rates are associated with greater Olympic success across European countries.
